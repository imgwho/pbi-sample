Magic Step 1: Translating "Data Model"

Magic Step 2: Conquering "LOD"

Tableau LOD (XML):


{FIXED [Order ID]:SUM([Profit])}>0

"OK, AI. Now translate *this* Tableau LOD expression."

AI-Generated DAX (TMDL):


measure 'Order Profitable' =
    VAR OrderProfit = CALCULATE(
        SUM(Orders[Profit]),
        ALLEXCEPT(Orders, Orders[Order ID])
    )
    RETURN IF(OrderProfit > 0, "Profitable", "Unprofitable")

Game-Changer. AI understands the *logic*.
